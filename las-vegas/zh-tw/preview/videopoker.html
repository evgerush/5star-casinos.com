Video Poker

Video Poker is very popular game in casinos around the world. We have created 15 different versions of Video Poker, each with their own unique pay table. You can choose to play with coin values of $0.05 to $5.00.

How to Play

The object of Video Poker is to obtain a five-card poker hand that contains at least the lowest combination on the pay table for the version you are playing. For this explanation we will use the version "Jacks or Better" to provide examples.

注: But remember, all 15 pay tables are different. In the case of "Jacks or Better", the lowest paying combination is a pair of Jacks or better.

To begin select the value of the coins you wish to wager: $0.05, $0.10, $0.25, $0.50, $1, $2 or $5. Then, select the number of these coins you wish to bet on your hand by clicking the left mouse button over the "BET 1" button up to five times, once you get to the fifth coin your hand is automatically dealt. If you wish to bet the max amount of coins on your hand, simply click the "MAX" button with your left mouse button. When you press the "MAX" button, your hand is automatically dealt. (Your odds of winning are maximized on max bet). If you use the "BET 1" button to select the number of coins you wish to wager, then you must use the "DEAL" button to start the game.

Once you are dealt your first cards, with the left mouse button, click on the cards you wish to hold, and you will see "HOLD" light up under the appropriate card. You can hold form 0 - 5 cards. When you have selected the cards you wish to hold click on the "DRAW" button. Upon clicking "DRAW" you will receive new cards for all the cards that you did not select to hold.

The main idea is to better your initial hand by holding your best cards and hopefully drawing new cards that better your held cards.
